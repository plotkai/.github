We are **Plotkai Interactive**, an independent mobile app development studio based in Lucknow, India. We specialize in building engaging, utility-driven, and educational Android applications tailored for diverse user needs — from local community networking to high-stakes mental math.

## 🚀 About Us

- 📱 **What we do:** Craft high-quality, offline-first, and experiential mobile applications.
- 💡 **Our Philosophy:** We believe in building software that delivers immediate value—whether it's mastering math through pressure and play, or discovering local freelancers in your city.
- 🌱 **Currently exploring:** Expanding our mobile ecosystem and pushing the boundaries of interactive learning and local networking.

## 📱 Our Featured Apps

### [Remainder™ - Math Survival](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.plotkai.remainder)
A hardcore mental math survival game that turns division into a high-stakes race against time. Built on the principles of experiential learning, it features procedural level scaling, strategic "Divider Toolkits," and unlocking amazing calculation facts used by math geniuses.

### [Chowk – Local Creators & Jobs](https://www.appbrain.com/app/chowk-local-creators-jobs/com.plotkai.chowk)
A localized social app that helps you find, hire, and connect with verified local creators, freelancers, and service providers right in your city.

### [Modern Hindu Baby Names](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.plotkai.modernBabyNames)
A lightweight, 100% offline utility app featuring over 50,000 modern GenZ names, allowing parents to search by gender, region, and inspiration to find the perfect name for their little one.
